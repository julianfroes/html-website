Cinematic Universes

The Lord of the Rings

The Lord of the Rings is a fantasy film series directed by Peter Jackson, based on the novels by J.R.R. Tolkien. It depicts the epic journey to destroy the One Ring and defeat the Dark Lord Sauron. The series consists of three films: The Fellowship of the Ring, The Two Towers, and The Return of the King.(My favorite movie)

Godzilla

Godzilla is a Japanese film series featuring the giant monster Godzilla, often battling other giant monsters and various human threats.

Kung Fu Panda

Kung Fu Panda is an animated film series produced by DreamWorks Animation. It follows the adventures of Po, a clumsy panda who becomes the Dragon Warrior and protects the Valley of Peace from various villains. The series is known for its humor, heartwarming moments, and impressive martial arts animation(Po > Shrek).
